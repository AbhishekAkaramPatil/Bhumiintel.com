Gamifying Accountability · Concept Preview

JanVaani Civic Score Index

A live, ward-wise and city-wise leaderboard that scores civic performance — combining citizen participation and civic body responsiveness into a single, comparable number. Illustrative concept — in development.

One score. Two sides of the story.

The Civic Score Index blends citizen engagement — reports filed, confirmations, active reporters per ward — with civic body performance — resolution speed, SLA adherence, before/after evidence rate. Wards compete against wards. Cities compete against cities.

This is not a vanity metric. A rising score is visible proof of governance improving. A falling score is public pressure that no press release can hide from.
